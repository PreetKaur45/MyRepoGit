Our background

Testicon is dedicated to helping people excel and succeed through certification. Our globally recognized credentials are aimed at enhancing individual productivity, marketability, and value.
Get certified and get noticed.

Who we are

Established in 2005, Testicon is a leader in providing performance-based certification exams for Professionals and students who want to start a carrer. We have 5 Testicon Authorized Testing Centers in Sweden and Denmark.

In the highly-competitive IT world, it is important that a programmer can verify his/her skills to future and current employers. We offer more than 10 professional IT certification exams from leading software, hardware, networking, web service companies and professional organizations.

Business Certificates are ideal for individuals, such as those who plan to pursue an MBA and professionals who want to enhance their knowledge in a specific area of business like marketing, finance, management etc.

If you have decided to become an independant consultant or a freelancer, you can impress your clients with your skills and dedication by getting certified.
We offer 5 consulting certifications that would be excellent additions to your resume like Project Management certification, SharePoint.

Testicon currently delivers more than 6500 certification exams each year.

What we do

Testicon works with industry-leading certification providers to bring their programs successfully to market.

With a particular expertise in academia, Testicon provides Professionals and students who want to start a carrer with oppurtunity to take certification in their respective fields of expertise. In the highly-competitive job market, it is important that an employee can verify his/her skills to future and current employers. We provide them with a platform to get highly-desired certifications for a brilliant career in IT, business or consulting services.

How we do it

Testicon delivers certification exams through a specialized network of Testicon Authorized Testing Centers in Sweden.

Centers are uniquely positioned to provide the highest level of reliable, performance-based testing.
